Explore Iconic Planets

Travel to legendary Star Wars locations like Tatooine, Alderaan, and Hoth. Each planet offers quests, lore, and enemies to conquer.

Some notable planets include:

  • Korriban - Ancient Sith homeworld
  • Coruscant - Galactic Republic capital
  • Ilum - Rich with crystals and Jedi lore
  • Tython - Homeworld of the Jedi Order (Republic)
  • Ord Mantell - War-torn planet, starting zone for Troopers and Smugglers (Republic)
  • Korriban - Ancient Sith world, starting zone for Sith Warriors and Inquisitors (Empire)
  • Hutta - Swampy world controlled by the Hutts, starting zone for Bounty Hunters and Imperial Agents (Empire)
