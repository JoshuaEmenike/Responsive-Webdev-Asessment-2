Welcome to the SWOTOR Universe

Star Wars: The Old Republic (SWOTOR) is a multiplayer online RPG set in the rich Star Wars galaxy. Choose your side — Jedi or Sith — and dive into epic stories, galactic battles, and immersive character development.

Explore famous planets, unlock new abilities, and fight for your chosen faction. Whether you're a lone bounty hunter or a heroic knight, the galaxy is yours to shape.
