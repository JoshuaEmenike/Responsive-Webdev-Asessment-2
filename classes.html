Character Classes

In SWOTOR, you can choose from 8 base classes, each with unique storylines and abilities. Classes are divided between the Galactic Republic and the Sith Empire.

  • Jedi Knight
  • Sith Juggernaught
  • Jedi Consular
  • Smuggler / Imperial Agent
  • Trooper / Bounty Hunter
